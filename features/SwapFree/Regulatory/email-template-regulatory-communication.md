## Instructions for Use:

2. **Share the document** with appropriate permissions (view-only for regulators, or comment access if you want feedback)
3. **Get the shareable link** from Google Docs
4. **Replace [INSERT GOOGLE DOCS LINK HERE]** in the email template with the actual link
5. **Fill in the contact information** placeholders ([To be filled])
6. **Customize the email** based on your relationship with each regulatory authority
7. **Send individual emails** to each regulatory authority (FSA, FSCA, FCA, ASIC, DFSA, CySec, SCA)

---

## Tips for Sending:

- **Personalize each email** - Address each regulator by their specific name/authority
- **Use appropriate tone** - Match the formality level expected by each regulator
- **Include all recipients** - Ensure compliance and legal teams are CC'd
- **Follow up** - Consider a follow-up email if no response is received within 2-3 weeks
- **Track responses** - Maintain a log of which regulators have acknowledged receipt
